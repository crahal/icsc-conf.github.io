Information for Sponsors

ICSC 2026 welcomes sponsorship from organisations interested in supporting research and practice in social computing. Sponsors will be acknowledged on conference materials and website communications.

Why sponsor ICSC 2026?

ICSC brings together researchers, students, and practitioners working across social computing, online communities, human-centred AI, and digital society. Sponsorship offers visibility within an international academic and professional audience.

Sponsorship information

Organisations interested in sponsoring the conference are warmly invited to contact the organising committee at icsc26@demography.ox.ac.uk.
