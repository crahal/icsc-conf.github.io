ICSC 2025 List of Accepted Papers

This year’s program will feature a diverse range of topics, highlighting the latest advancements in collaborative research at the intersection of big data analytics and social science theory. To offer a preview of the depth and excellence of this work, we are pleased to announce the acceptance of the following papers:
